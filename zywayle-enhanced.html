💡 How It Works

Zywayle trains a word-level neural language model in your browser.

Each word is mapped to a learned embedding vector. A hidden ReLU layer learns contextual patterns. Trained via backprop + SGD momentum.

At generation time, the model samples from a probability distribution over all known words conditioned on context. No APIs. No servers. Pure learned weights.
